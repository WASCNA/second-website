The Wheeling Area of Narcotics Anonymous—WASCNA—created an Area Inventory Ad Hoc Subcommittee to create this survey with the intent to better serve the groups and members in the area. It is anonymous, and can be completed here online or there will be identical paper copies distributed widely across the area and at the December 16 Holiday Dinner Dance, but please only complete the survey once, either here online or on paper.

To utitilize the free services available online, this survery had to be created in 2 parts, please DO complete both parts of the survey. Each part only takes 2-3 minutes to complete.
