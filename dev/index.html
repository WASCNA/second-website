Welcome!

Ohio Valley Recovery, Inc. seeks to serve the community of people recovering from substance abuse by providing a venue that fosters fellowship and supports group meetings of a program-oriented or social nature. We offer a secure setting for entertainment and recreation, serve as a center for communication of information concerning events in the recovering community, and host various retail enterprises targeted to the recovering community.

OVRI sponsors The Unity Center, a multipurpose facility for use by various substance abuse rehabilitation programs. It is not a treatment center, nor does it have an affiliation with any specific organization. Instead, it provides a facility for various groups to hold meetings. It also houses a coffee shop for use by session members during and after meetings, as well as any time during operation.

In addition, it is a social gathering place that will offer memberships to individuals whether or not their groups meet there. Stop by and check us out!
